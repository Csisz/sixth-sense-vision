# Wow Effect Guidelines

Mission

Create screenshots people want to share.

---

The image itself is the product.

---

First reaction

"Wow."

Second reaction

"I want this."

Third reaction

"I need to show this to someone."

---

Do not optimize for

Accuracy.

Complexity.

Density.

Reports.

---

Optimize for

Beauty.

Emotion.

Wonder.

Curiosity.

---

The user should feel

Like they have acquired a new sense.

---

Questions

Would this screenshot work on Twitter?

Would somebody send this to a friend?

Would YouTube thumbnails look amazing?

Would people say:

"How did your phone do that?"

---

Avoid

Technical jargon.

Huge paragraphs.

Enterprise charts.

Boxes everywhere.

Red rectangles.

---

Prefer

Heatmaps.

Neon outlines.

Soft glow.

Transparent overlays.

Sci-fi atmosphere.

Subtle labels.

---

Golden rule

People forgive mistakes.

People do not forgive boring.

---

The image should feel

More Iron Man.

Less Excel.
