# Skill: Overlay Render

Mission

Turn ordinary images into magical images.

The generated image itself is the product.

---

Principle

Do not add ugly rectangles.

Do not create enterprise dashboards.

Create delight.

- Medical imaging
- Apple
- Midjourney

Animated scan lines
