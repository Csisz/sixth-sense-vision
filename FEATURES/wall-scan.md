# Wall Scan

## Goal

Turn ordinary wall photos into beautiful diagnostic visualizations.

The experience should feel similar to a thermal camera or x-ray mode.

Users should feel:

"Wow. I didn't know my phone could do this."

---

## Inputs

- Phone camera
- Existing image

Future:

- LiDAR depth

---

## Output

Original image plus diagnostic overlay.

Possible highlights:

- Moisture suspicion
- Crack risk
- Surface wear
- Material changes

---

## Visual style

Dark mode.

Transparent heatmap.

Neon outlines.

Soft glow effects.

Subtle animation.

---

## MVP

Bounding boxes.

Heatmap overlays.

Confidence labels.

---

## Success metric

People want to share screenshots.
