# Shoe Scan

## Goal

Analyze shoe wear patterns.

Provide beautiful visual feedback.

---

## Inputs

Sole photo.

---

## Outputs

Pressure estimation.

Wear map.

Left-right asymmetry.

Potential posture issues.

---

## Visual style

Heatmap.

Red:

Heavy wear.

Green:

Healthy wear.

Blue:

Low pressure.

---

## Target users

Runners.

Parents.

Fitness enthusiasts.

---

## User feeling

"This is like an MRI for my shoes."
