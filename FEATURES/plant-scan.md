# Plant Scan

## Goal

Create an x-ray style view of plants.

---

## Inputs

Leaf photo.

Fruit photo.

Whole plant.

---

## Outputs

Possible:

- Water stress
- Disease
- Nutrient deficiency

---

## Visual style

Living heatmap.

Glowing veins.

Organic colors.

---

## User feeling

"My plants are talking to me."
