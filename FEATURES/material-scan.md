# Material Scan

## Goal

Reveal what objects are made of.

Not object recognition.

Material recognition.

---

## Detectable materials

- Wood
- Stone
- Concrete
- Metal
- Plastic

---

## Future states

- Rust
- Wear
- Rot
- Moisture

---

## Visual experience

Sci-fi forensic style.

Different colors represent materials.

Example:

Green:

Healthy wood.

Orange:

Suspicious area.

Red:

Potential decay.

---

## User feeling

"My phone sees more than I do."

---

## MVP

Photo upload.

Material segmentation.

Overlay rendering.
